A brand for government to citizen interaction

Reinvention. That’s the mission of MyUSA, one of five new ‘game changing’ projects part of the Presidential Innovation Fellows program.

I served as a member of the inaugural class of fellows, working on the visual development, UX and product design for all things MyUSA. A central challenge: Develop a brand that defies the typical associations we have with Government.

MyUSA went from whiteboard sketch to private beta during our six month fellowship, and is slated to launch in 2014.
